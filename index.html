Beef Biryani

One pot beef biryani infused with classic Indian spices.

INGREDIENTS

Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 dried bay leaf
  • 2 cardamom pods
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 10 cups water
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste

Fried Option:

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 cups sliced white onions

Fried raisins:

  • 1 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 cup raisins
